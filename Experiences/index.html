# Here are some some fun expreiences that you can expect while visiting the beautiful Island Of Mauritius.

# Some the top things to do in Mauritius, from beaches to botanical gardens.

  1. Visit Ile aux Cerfs

Img source -Ile au Cerfsopen in new window

Ile Aux Cerfs is famous for its sandy beaches, beautiful lagoon and big selection of activities and facilities. In Ile Aux Cerfs you are sure to have a day of relaxation and fun on one of the picture-postcard beaches, and to enjoy a swim and snorkeling in the lagoon.

  1. Les 7 Cascades

Img source -7 Cascadesopen in new window

Les 7 Cascades meaning 'Seven Waterfalls', is the commonly used name for the Tamarin Waterfalls. It is found near the small Mauritian south-western village of Henrietta. It emerges from the Riviere Tamarin and takes the form of seven simultaneous falls.

  1. La Vanille Nature Park

Img source -La Vanille Nature Park open in new window

At La Vanille Nature Park you will discover the flora and fauna endemic to Mauritius and the neighbouring islands.You will be introduced to a collection of fossils of animals that are now extinct in Mauritius and surrounding islands such as the dodo and the elephant bird of Madagascar. You will get the opportunity to learn about insects as well since La Vanille Nature Park also has an insectarium with a rare collection of species from various locations around the world.

  1. L'Aventure Du Sucre

Img source -L'Aventure Du Sucre open in new window

The former Beau Plan sugar factory houses one of the best museums in Mauritius. It not only tells the story of sugar in great detail but also covers the history of Mauritius, slavery and the rum trade.

  1. Chamarel Seven Coloured Earth Geopark

Img source -Chamarel Seven Coloured Earth Geoparkopen in new window

The main attraction is the Chamarel Seven Coloured Earth Geopark where you can witness the different coloured earths and leann about their formation and composition. Head to the park and see the rainbow-hued sand dunes surrounded by endemic plant & species.
